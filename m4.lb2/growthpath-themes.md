# GrowthPath Presentation Themes

## Brand Theme Specifications

Use these specifications when setting up Gamma themes or instructing AI to apply consistent styling.

---

## Primary Theme: "Edinburgh Professional"

### Colour Scheme

| Element | Colour | Hex | Usage |
|---------|--------|-----|-------|
| Primary Background | Off-White | #F7F5F3 | Most slide backgrounds |
| Accent Background | Deep Teal | #1A5F7A | Title slides, section dividers |
| Primary Text | Charcoal | #2D3748 | Body text |
| Headings | Deep Teal | #1A5F7A | All headlines |
| Accent | Warm Amber | #F2A900 | Highlights, callouts, icons |
| Secondary Accent | Soft Coral | #E8927C | Subtle emphasis |

### Typography

| Element | Font | Weight | Size Range |
|---------|------|--------|------------|
| Headlines | Montserrat | Bold | 36-48pt |
| Subheadings | Montserrat | SemiBold | 24-32pt |
| Body Text | Open Sans | Regular | 16-20pt |
| Captions | Open Sans | Light | 12-14pt |
| Quotes | Montserrat | Medium Italic | 20-28pt |

### Layout Principles

- **Generous white space**: Slides should breathe
- **Left-aligned text**: Easier to read than centred
- **Consistent margins**: 10% padding on all sides
- **Visual hierarchy**: Clear distinction between heading/subhead/body
- **Single focus**: One key message per slide

## Secondary Theme: "Growth Warmth"

For more casual presentations - team updates, internal communications, culture content.

### Colour Scheme

| Element | Colour | Hex | Usage |
|---------|--------|-----|-------|
| Primary Background | Warm White | #FBF9F7 | Most slides |
| Accent Background | Soft Coral | #E8927C | Feature slides |
| Primary Text | Slate Grey | #4A5568 | Body text |
| Headings | Deep Teal | #1A5F7A | Headlines |
| Accent | Warm Amber | #F2A900 | Highlights |

### Layout Principles

- Same typography as Edinburgh Professional
- More photography, less iconography
- Warmer, more approachable feeling
- Still professional, but friendlier

---

## Slide Type Templates

### Title Slides

- Full-bleed Edinburgh imagery as background
- Dark overlay (50% opacity deep teal) for text contrast
- White headline, off-white subhead
- GrowthPath logo bottom-right corner

### Content Slides

- Clean white/off-white background
- Teal headline, charcoal body
- Amber accents for emphasis
- Optional right-rail imagery

### Section Dividers

- Deep teal background
- White headline centred
- Minimal content - just section name
- Amber accent line below title

### Case Study Slides

- Split layout: narrative left, metrics right
- Photo of client/project as subtle background
- Quote highlighted in amber box
- Clear before/after comparison where applicable

### Data/Chart Slides

- White background for chart clarity
- Chart uses brand colour palette
- Key insight called out in amber
- Source cited in caption text

### Quote Slides

- Centred large quote in teal italic
- Attribution below in regular weight
- Optional subtle background imagery
- Generous white space

### CTA/Next Steps Slides

- White background, clear action items
- Numbered steps in amber circles
- Primary CTA in amber highlight box
- Contact details clearly displayed

## Gamma-Specific Instructions

When prompting Gamma to create GrowthPath presentations:

### Theme Application Prompt
```
Apply a professional theme with:
- Primary colour: deep teal (#1A5F7A)
- Accent colour: warm amber (#F2A900)
- Background: off-white (#F7F5F3)
- Clean, minimalist layout with generous white space
- Headlines in bold, left-aligned
- Professional but approachable tone
```

### Style Guidance Prompt
```
Visual style should be:
- Modern and clean, not busy or cluttered
- Professional but warmer than typical corporate
- Edinburgh/Scottish references where appropriate
- Photography-forward rather than icon-heavy
- Consistent with a boutique consultancy, not Big Four
```

### What to Avoid Prompt
```
Do not include:
- Generic stock photo handshakes
- Cheesy business metaphors (arrows up, mountains, puzzle pieces)
- Overcrowded slides with too much text
- Clip art or dated graphics
- Harsh contrasts or neon accents
```

## Quality Checklist

Before finalising any GrowthPath presentation:

- [ ] Colours match brand palette exactly
- [ ] Typography is consistent throughout
- [ ] White space is generous and balanced
- [ ] Each slide has one clear message
- [ ] Headlines are action-oriented, not vague
- [ ] Edinburgh imagery feels authentic, not cliche
- [ ] Overall tone is professional but approachable
- [ ] No spelling or grammar errors (British English)
- [ ] GrowthPath logo appears on final slide
- [ ] Contact information is current and accurate
