## Key Takeaways

1. **Projects have end dates; Areas don't**
2. **Inbox is temporary** - process it, don't let things live there
3. **Resources support other work** - they're not active themselves
4. **Archives are for completed/inactive items** - not a dumping ground
5. **When in doubt, ask:** "Will this ever be done?"
