## Colour Palette

| Colour | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|--------|-----|-----|-------|
| Deep Teal | #1A5F7A | 26, 95, 122 | Primary brand colour, headers, key elements |
| Warm Amber | #F2A900 | 242, 169, 0 | Accent, highlights, calls to action |

### Secondary Colours

| Colour | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|--------|-----|-----|-------|
| Slate Grey | #4A5568 | 74, 85, 104 | Body text, secondary elements |
| Soft Coral | #E8927C | 232, 146, 124 | Warm accents, approachable touches |
| Off-White | #F7F5F3 | 247, 245, 243 | Backgrounds, breathing room |

### Colour Relationships

- **Teal + Amber**: High contrast, energy and trust
- **Teal + Coral**: Softer, more approachable
- **Slate + Off-White**: Professional, clean backgrounds

## Visual Identity

### Mood & Tone

**We are**:
- Professional but approachable
- Scottish with global ambition
- Modern without being trendy
- Warm and trustworthy
- Growth-focused without being aggressive

**We are not**:
- Corporate-sterile
- Overly casual or unprofessional
- Generic or forgettable
- Cold or intimidating
- Flashy or gimmicky

### Photography Style

**Preferred**:
- Natural lighting, golden hour
- Edinburgh and Scottish landscapes (authentic, not tourist-cliche)
- Real workspaces with character
- Warm colour temperature
- Clean compositions with negative space

**Avoid**:
- Harsh flash photography
- Generic stock photo aesthetics
- Overcrowded compositions
- Cold, blue-tinted images
- Obviously posed or fake scenarios

### Graphic Elements

**Preferred**:
- Organic shapes and flowing lines
- Subtle gradients in brand colours
- Natural textures (stone, wood, paper)
- Clean geometric accents

**Avoid**:
- Sharp, aggressive angles
- Busy patterns
- Clip art or outdated graphics
- Neon or fluorescent colours

## Brand Voice in Imagery

### What GrowthPath Images Should Communicate

1. **Trust**: We're experienced and reliable
2. **Growth**: We help businesses improve
3. **Scottish Roots**: We're proud of our Edinburgh heritage
4. **Modern Approach**: We use contemporary methods
5. **Partnership**: We work alongside clients, not above them

### Visual Metaphors We Like

- **Paths and journeys**: Growth as a process
- **Edinburgh landmarks**: Rooted in Scotland
- **Natural growth**: Plants, organic forms
- **Light and clarity**: Transformation and understanding
- **Collaboration**: Working together, shared spaces

### Visual Metaphors We Avoid

- **Arrows and charts**: Too cliche
- **Handshakes**: Overused in business imagery
- **Globes**: We're Scottish-focused, not generic global
- **Mountains**: Overused "achievement" metaphor
- **Puzzle pieces**: Outdated teamwork metaphor

## Application Examples

### Investor Presentation Header
Deep teal as dominant colour, amber accent lighting, Edinburgh skyline subtle in background, clean and confident composition

### Blog Post Feature
Warm amber tones, growth metaphor through organic shapes, plenty of negative space for text overlay, 16:9 ratio

### Social Media Posts
Square format, warm and approachable, team/culture focus, natural lighting, coral accents for friendliness

### Case Study Visuals
Before/after progression implied, transformation theme, professional environment, teal-to-amber colour journey

## File Naming Convention

When saving generated images:

```
[purpose]-[description]-[version].png

Examples:
blog-header-strategy-reset-v1.png
social-team-culture-v2.png
pitch-deck-hero-edinburgh-final.png
```

Save all generated imagery to: `brand-images/`
Document successful prompts in: `successful-prompts.md`
