### Where We Are

GrowthPath has transformed completely: organised knowledge vault (Module 1), content processing capabilities (Module 2), and professional document generation skills (Module 3). Now they're ready to scale their output by connecting to external AI services through MCP (Model Context Protocol) servers.

### The Opportunity

GrowthPath wants to compete with larger agencies that have dedicated design, video, and audio production teams. With MCP integrations, a four-person consultancy can produce:
- AI-generated brand imagery
- Rich interactive presentations
- Professional audio content (podcasts, voiceovers)
- AI avatar videos for marketing
- Multi-platform social media campaigns

### Primary Persona

**Isla Murray** leads all of Module 4. As Marketing Manager, she's been frustrated that GrowthPath can't produce the visual and multimedia content that larger competitors deliver. MCP integrations unlock capabilities she's only dreamed of.

By the end of Module 4, GrowthPath can:

### Free Tier Considerations

Most services offer free tiers suitable for learning:
- **Nano Banana Pro** (Gemini): Generous free quota
- **ElevenLabs**: 10,000 characters/month (~10 minutes audio)
- **HeyGen**: Limited free credits
- **Canva**: Free tier with watermarks

For production use, paid tiers would be required. The course focuses on demonstrating capabilities within free tier limits.
